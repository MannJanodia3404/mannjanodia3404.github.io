// ABOUT

Why I build robots

I specialize in full-stack robotics: the seamless integration of mechanical hardware, control software and artificial intelligence. I am driven by the moment these disciplines converge, whether that is an autonomous navigation stack or a custom neural network for adaptive flight. I do not just write code, I build complete systems.

My background includes award winning, patent pending self-balancing systems and vision-based specimen handling platforms using high-precision depth sensing. I am interested in narrowing the gap between what robots can do and what we need them to do.

Off the lab floor: competitive cricket, football, volleyball, chess and a steady stack of books.
